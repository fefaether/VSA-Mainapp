## Overview

VirtualLens Controller adjusts VirtualLens2 camera parameters in real time over OSC. Use sliders for focal length, aperture, exposure, and more, and save presets.

## How to open

1. Open **Controller** in the sidebar
2. Confirm VirtualLens2 and OSC are enabled in VRChat
3. Check OSC settings in [Game Config](game-config.md) if needed

## Main operations

### Screen overview

The controller shows parameters, presets, and OSC status.

![Controller overview](../../assets/screenshots/controller-overview.webp)

### Live controls

Changing sliders or numeric values updates VRChat while connected. Save and load common combinations as presets.

![Live controls](../../assets/screenshots/controller-live-controls.webp)

## Notes

- Changes do nothing if OSC is disconnected or the port is wrong
- Cameras other than VirtualLens2 may not behave as expected
- VSA is not affiliated with or endorsed by VirtualLens2 or its rights holders
